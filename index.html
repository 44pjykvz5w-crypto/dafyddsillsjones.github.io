Artist · Filmmaker · Researcher

Documentary, virtual production, AI and place.

I explore documentary, virtual production and AI as practices through which places, languages and communities continue to shape digital media. My work moves between Wales and Aotearoa New Zealand, asking how emerging technologies can remain accountable to the places from which they emerge.

Portals / Traces

From landscape to scan to performance.

Portals/Traces develops shared virtual production workflows across real places, scanned environments, live mocap, AI image systems and networked performance. The central question is simple: what happens when virtual production remains accountable to the place from which it emerges?
