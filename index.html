Research

My research explores relationships between place, memory, language and emerging forms of screen practice.

Virtual Production Virtual production, real-time engines, motion capture, photogrammetry and the ethics of digital environments.
Documentary and AI AI, machine vision, authorship, evidence and new documentary languages.
Minority-language Media Welsh-language screen cultures, documentary voice and linguistic visibility.
